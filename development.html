The Development Process

FIVE PHASES.
ZERO SURPRISES.

01

Plot of Land

Zoning, utilities, soil — every lot is fully underwritten before a purchase offer is made. The right land is the foundation of every return.

02

Foundation

Excavation, forms, rebar, concrete pour and cure. The first dollars spent set the quality and precision standard for everything above grade.

03

Framing

Walls, floors, and roof trusses framed with daily on-site oversight. Deviations caught and corrected before they become expensive change orders.

04

Windows, Roof & Siding

Windows, doors, roofing, and siding installed to seal the envelope. Curb appeal locked in and the building made weather-tight for interior work.

05

Landscaping

Final grading, sod, plantings, driveway, and walkways complete the project. Certificate of Occupancy obtained and investor returns distributed.
