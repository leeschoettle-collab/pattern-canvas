The Flip Process

HOW EVERY
DEAL IS RUN

01

Deal Analysis

Every potential flip is underwritten before an offer is made. ARV, rehab scope, holding costs, and exit timing — all stress-tested before a single dollar is committed.

02

Acquisition

Off-market sourcing, distressed sellers, estate sales — Lee's negotiation skills find the spread before competitors see the listing.

03

Scope & Budget

A detailed scope of work is finalized before closing. Every line item is priced, every sub-trade is vetted. No surprises during the rehab.

04

Project Management

Daily oversight of contractors. Milestone-based payment schedules. Lee's system keeps crews moving and budgets intact from demo to punch list.

05

Staging & List

Strategic staging, professional photography, and precision pricing — the exit is planned from day one and executed to maximize sale price.

06

Close & Distribute

Clean title, fast closing. Proceeds distributed to investors with a full deal debrief — because the next deal starts where the last one finished.
